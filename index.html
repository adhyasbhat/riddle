GUESS THE WORD

TOTAL TIME TAKEN :

Description :

  • Its a Guess a word (Hangman) game
  • Based on the given hint you need to guess the word
  • You get 1 point on each correct guess
  • There are 3 levels
    • In 1st level you need to guess 3 letter word, similarly 4 letters word in 2nd Level and 5 letters word in final level
    • In 1st level you will be having 2 life, in 2nd and final level you will be having 3 life
    • You will go to next level only if you pass the present level
  • There is no negative point for wrong guess
  • You will have 20sec to guess each word
    • If you dont guess within given time it will automatically go to next question
  • You can restart the game at any point of time

All the best !!!
